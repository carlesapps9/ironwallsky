Privacy Policy

Iron Wall Sky  ·  Effective date: March 3, 2026  ·  Last updated: March 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Iron Wall Sky (the "App") handles information when you play the game. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII). The App has no user accounts, no login, and no forms that gather your name, email address, or any other personal data.

2. Advertising (Google AdMob)

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use the following data to serve and measure ads:

  • Device advertising identifier (IDFA on iOS, GAID on Android)
  • Coarse location derived from IP address (country / region level)
  • App interaction data (ad impressions, clicks, conversion events)
  • Device information (OS version, screen size, device model)

This data is collected and processed by Google, not by us. Google's data practices are governed by the Google Privacy Policy .

On iOS 14.5+, you will be shown an App Tracking Transparency (ATT) prompt before any advertising identifier is accessed. You may deny tracking and the App will continue to function normally; you will receive non-personalised ads.

On Android, you can opt out of personalised ads at any time via Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalisation.

3. Local Storage

The App saves your high score and game preferences locally on your device using the browser's localStorage API (or IndexedDB as a fallback). This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

4. Analytics

The App may record anonymous, aggregated gameplay events (e.g. run completed, ad impression) solely for internal quality monitoring. No event is linked to an individual user and no PII is transmitted.

5. Children

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect any information from children. If you believe a child has provided information through the App, please contact us and we will promptly address it.

6. Data Retention

Because we do not collect personal data, there is nothing for us to retain or delete. AdMob's data retention is governed by Google's policies.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
